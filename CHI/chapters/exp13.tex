\section{Problem Definition}
To generate a Fibonacci series with the limit read as the input\\
\section{Algorithm}
1.Start\\
2.Point DPTR to memory location 4500\\
3.Move the count to register \\
4.Move 00 to A\\
5.Move A to memory location 4500\\
6.Move A to R1\\
7.Decrement count\\
8.Increment DPTR\\
9.Move 01 to A register, then to R2 register\\
10.Decrement count\\
11.Move R1 to A\\
12.Add A with R2\\
13.Move A to R3\\
14.Move content of DPTR to A, then move R2\\
15.Decrement DPTR\\
16.Move R3 to A, then move to the desired memory location\\
17.Decrement R0\\
18.If R0 is not zero,loop\\
19.Stop\\
\section{Program}
MOV DPTR,\#4200H\\
MOVX A,@DPTR\\
MOV R7,A\\
MOV A,\#00\\
MOV R1,\#01\\
MOV DPTR,\#4300H\\
LOOP1 :MOV @DPTR,A\\
INC DPTR\\
ADD A,R1\\
MOV R2,A\\
MOV A,R1\\
MOV R3,A\\
MOV A,R2\\
MOV R1,A\\
MOV A,R3\\
DJNZ R7,LOOP1\\
HLT :SJMP HLT\\
\section{Sample Inputs}
(4200): 07\\
\section{Sample Outputs}
(4300): 00 01 01 02 03 05 08\\
\section{Result}
Program executed successfully and the output is verified.
